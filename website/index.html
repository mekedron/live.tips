How it works

From soundcheck to first tip in three steps

account_balance

Connect your Stripe

Create a restricted API key in your own Stripe dashboard — two minutes, fully guided — and paste it in. The app creates your pay-what-you-want tip link automatically.

qr_code_2

Put one QR on stage

Print the poster, tape it to the guitar case, put it on the merch table. Fans scan, pick an amount, leave a name and a message — Stripe handles the payment.

celebration

Watch the jar fill live

Your tablet on stage shows every tip in real time against tonight's goal — 3D jar, messages, confetti. Edit the goal mid-set; lock the screen while it sits out front.

Security

Your keys, your money

There is no live.tips server between your fans and your bank account. The app talks to api.stripe.com — and nowhere else.

key

Bring your own key, keep your own key

The restricted key is stored in your device's keychain and only ever sent to Stripe over TLS. Full live secret keys are refused outright.

shield

Least privilege by design

The key can create your tip link and watch donations — nothing else. It cannot touch balances, payouts, refunds, or customer data.

account_balance_wallet

Refunds and payouts stay in Stripe

Money matters stay where they belong: in your own Stripe dashboard, under your own account.
